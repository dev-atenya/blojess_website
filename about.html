About Blojess
Enterprises

A proudly Kenyan general supply company built on teamwork, partnership, and an unwavering commitment to quality and integrity.

Who We Are

Dedicated to Supplying Kenya & Beyond

Blojess Enterprises is dedicated to providing facilities in Kenya with the latest and most advanced products across general supply — from fresh foods to technology, building materials to health consumables.

We are a strong believer in teamwork and partnership, and are proud to be associated with world-renowned suppliers and partners who work closely with us to develop and support our clients.

Our company strives to continuously provide the latest and most advanced supply of products and services in association with the most renowned suppliers and partners engaged in our markets.

🎯 Our Vision
To be a general supplier firm with the latest mechanisms that promote professionalism and integrity in providing excellent service to clients and partners.
🚀 Our Mission
To maintain excellence in supplying, training, implementation and support — while continuing to invest in high-quality staff and services, and managing through controlled growth and close strategic partnerships.
💪 Our Strength
A strong management team with versatile national and international exposure in the general supply industry across different sectors and scenarios.
Our Core Values

The Principles We Live By

These values guide every decision we make — from how we treat our staff and customers, to how we work with our suppliers and the communities we serve.

🏆
Excellence
We champion and promote individual and institutional excellence in Kenya, Africa and beyond — in everything we think, say or do.
⚖️
Integrity
We maintain the highest ethical and professional standards — honesty, truthfulness and decorum in all dealings with staff, customers and the community.
💡
Innovativeness
We encourage an environment that nurtures individual and institutional creativity, freshness of thought, and the spirit of exploration and discovery.
📋
Ethics & Compliance
We comply with applicable laws, professional ethics and company regulations at all government levels wherever we do business.
🌱
Community & Environment
We contribute to the communities in which we operate, address social issues responsibly, and conserve natural resources for a better environment.
👥
People First
We attract, motivate and retain staff with self-drive and determination — offering equal career opportunity, merit-based growth and continuous training.
Our Commitments
